Our Story

Baked with Intention, Delivered with Joy

What began as a passion for perfecting the classic red velvet in a Kilimani kitchen has blossomed into Nairobi's most-loved artisanal bakery. Every cake that leaves our kitchen carries a piece of our story.

We believe that premium ingredients and genuine craft make all the difference — from the Belgian couverture chocolate we fold into our ganache, to the hand-harvested vanilla beans in every sponge.
